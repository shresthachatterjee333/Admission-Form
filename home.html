MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

It is my pleasure to welcome students back to the college after two years of intermittent meetings and online
classes. Even as we adapted to the need of the hour, it is indeed a pleasure to have students back in our
brick-and-mortar classrooms and hear their young voices fill our corridors with laughter.
I am glad to inform you that our college has successfully completed the third NAAC cycle and has therefore
been re-accredited. Our institution has been praised for its social commitment and strong institutional values
that it seeks to instil in students. We firmly believe that our march towards a better future will be led by
our students in the years to come. My request to every incoming student is to explore the facilities that this
college offers you, from the extensive library to the outreach activities under the NSS and make the fullest
of the three years you will spend with us.

College

Andrew College, established in 1956,14 August; is an undergraduate college in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. It is affiliated with the University of Calcutta. The name commemorates the legacy of Sir Andrew the first Indian vice-chancellor of the University of Calcutta.
